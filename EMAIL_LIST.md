# Email Notifications List

## Emails Sent to Sellers

1. **Instruction Request Notification**
   - **Action:** Agent requests property instruction
   - **Subject:** "Action Required: Sign Your Terms & Conditions - [Property Address]"
   - **Purpose:** Seller needs to sign terms & conditions

2. **Welcome Pack**
   - **Action:** Seller completes property instruction (signs T&C)
   - **Subject:** "Welcome to Abodeology - Your Welcome Pack"
   - **Purpose:** Welcome email with dashboard access

3. **Post-Valuation Email**
   - **Action:** Valuation appointment is completed
   - **Subject:** "Post-Valuation Follow-Up - [Property Address]"
   - **Purpose:** Follow-up to encourage proceeding with instruction

4. **Valuation Login Credentials**
   - **Action:** New seller account created during valuation booking
   - **Subject:** "Welcome to Abodeology - Your Login Credentials"
   - **Purpose:** Sends login credentials to new account

5. **New Offer Notification**
   - **Action:** Buyer submits an offer on seller's property
   - **Subject:** "New Offer Received - [Property Address]" (or with amount if released)
   - **Purpose:** Notifies seller about new offer

6. **Offer Amount Released**
   - **Action:** Admin releases offer amount to seller
   - **Subject:** "Offer Amount Released - £[Amount] - [Property Address]"
   - **Purpose:** Notifies seller that offer amount is now visible

7. **Memorandum of Sale**
   - **Action:** Offer accepted and Memorandum of Sale generated
   - **Subject:** "Memorandum of Sale - [Property Address]"
   - **Purpose:** Sends Memorandum of Sale PDF document

8. **Memorandum Pending Info**
   - **Action:** Memorandum of Sale generated but seller information is incomplete
   - **Subject:** "Action Required: Complete Your Information for Memorandum of Sale"
   - **Purpose:** Requests missing information

9. **Property Status Changed**
   - **Action:** Property status changes (sold, withdrawn, or SSTC)
   - **Subject:** "Property Has Been Sold/Withdrawn/SSTC - [Property Address]"
   - **Purpose:** Notifies seller about status change

10. **Viewing Confirmed**
    - **Action:** Viewing appointment is confirmed/scheduled
    - **Subject:** "Viewing Confirmed for Your Property - [Property Address]"
    - **Purpose:** Confirms viewing appointment details

---

## Emails Sent to Buyers

1. **Offer Decision Notification**
   - **Action:** Seller makes decision on buyer's offer (accepted/declined/counter)
   - **Subject:** 
     - Accepted: "Great News! Your Offer Has Been Accepted"
     - Declined: "Offer Response - [Property Address]"
     - Counter: "Counter-Offer Discussion Request - [Property Address]"
   - **Purpose:** Notifies buyer about offer decision

2. **Memorandum of Sale**
   - **Action:** Offer accepted and Memorandum of Sale generated
   - **Subject:** "Memorandum of Sale - [Property Address]"
   - **Purpose:** Sends Memorandum of Sale PDF document

3. **Memorandum Pending Info**
   - **Action:** Memorandum of Sale generated but buyer information is incomplete
   - **Subject:** "Action Required: Complete Your Information for Memorandum of Sale"
   - **Purpose:** Requests missing information

4. **Viewing Confirmed**
   - **Action:** Viewing appointment is confirmed/scheduled
   - **Subject:** "Viewing Confirmed - [Property Address]"
   - **Purpose:** Confirms viewing appointment details

---

## Emails Sent to Agents/Admins

1. **Valuation Request Notification**
   - **Action:** New valuation appointment is requested
   - **Subject:** "New Valuation Appointment Request - [Property Address]"
   - **Purpose:** Notifies agents/admins about new valuation booking

2. **New Offer Notification**
   - **Action:** Buyer submits an offer
   - **Subject:** "New Offer Received - £[Amount] - [Property Address]"
   - **Purpose:** Notifies agent about new offer

3. **Offer Discussion Request**
   - **Action:** Seller requests discussion about an offer
   - **Subject:** "[Priority] Seller Discussion Request - Offer on [Property Address]"
   - **Purpose:** Notifies agent about seller's discussion request

---

## Emails Sent to PVAs (Property Viewing Agents)

1. **Viewing Request Notification**
   - **Action:** Buyer requests a property viewing
   - **Subject:** "New Viewing Request - [Property Address]"
   - **Purpose:** Notifies all PVAs about new viewing request

2. **Viewing Assigned**
   - **Action:** Viewing is assigned to a specific PVA
   - **Subject:** "New Viewing Assigned - [Property Address]"
   - **Purpose:** Notifies PVA about viewing assignment

3. **PVA Account Created**
   - **Action:** Admin creates a new PVA account
   - **Subject:** "Welcome to Abodeology - Your PVA Account"
   - **Purpose:** Sends login credentials to new PVA account
